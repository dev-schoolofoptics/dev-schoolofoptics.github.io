Our Mission

Welcome to the School Of Optics, founded in 2023 by Mohammed Bilal Saleem and Najaf Shah.

Our mission is to support students and newly qualified professionals in the field of Optics, guiding them through their educational journey and helping them make informed career choices. Based in West Yorkshire, we currently offer mock OSCE workshops, with plans to expand into specialized tutorials on slit-lamp skills, pre-registration competency workshops, and webinars tailored for newly qualified Optometrists. Let us help you excel in your Optical Career!
