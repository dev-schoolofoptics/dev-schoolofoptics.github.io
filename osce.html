What Will You Get From Our OSCE Workshop?

You will gain invaluable experience and insight into what an actual OSCE exam feels like. Our comprehensive mock OSCE session is designed to closely mimic the real examination environment, complete with multiple stations that cover a range of skills and scenarios. This hands-on practice will not only familiarize you with the format but also boost your confidence, helping you feel fully prepared and ready to excel when the real exam comes around.
Our assessors will also provide feedback for each station. We will then dissect the OSCE as a group. Covering each of the competencies, Data Interpretation, communication case hx and practical skills. This will give you an understanding of how to tackle each section and what the examiners are looking for. We will then follow up with a calculation station covering the math needed for the OSCE.
